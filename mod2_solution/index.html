My Favorite Games

Terraria

Terraria is a land of adventure! A land of mystery! A land that's yours to shape, defend, and enjoy. Your options in Terraria are limitless. Start by building basic shelter, then dig for Ore and other resources. Discover and craft up to 300 weapons of magic, ranged, and melee varieties, as well as armor, and use them to battle hundreds of different enemies. (WiKi)

Don't Starve

Don't Starve is an action-adventure game with a randomly generated open world and elements of survival and roguelike gameplay. The goal is to survive as long as possible, with a count of the number of days the player has survived shown onscreen. (Wiki)

Dark Souls

Dark Souls is a third-person action role-playing game. The core mechanic of the game is exploration. Players are encouraged by the game to proceed with caution, learn from past mistakes, or find alternate areas to explore. Dark Souls takes place in a large and continuous open world environment, connected through a central hub area (WiKi).
